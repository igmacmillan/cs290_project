Mission:

The purpose of this site is to pass along my enjoyment of spirits. This site is by no means meant to illustrate the "correct" or "proper" way to enjoy a spirit. As a famous fake internet judge says: "People like what they like".
To further this sites purpose feedback is included right on the homepage. If you have suggestions related to design or content, or if you just want to pass along well wishes please feel free to provide those here. I only ask that you keep the suggestions civil and that they would be constructive suggestions you would want to hear in my place.
